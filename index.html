Computational Science Tracks

All participants will explore these four fundamental computational science tracks during the hackathon:

Computational Physics

Dive into physical simulations and solve complex physics problems using computational methods.

  • Molecular Dynamics
  • Quantum Mechanics
  • Particle Physics
  • N-body Simulations

Computational Biology

Model biological systems and analyze genomic data using computational approaches.

  • Protein Folding
  • Gene Sequencing
  • Systems Biology
  • Bioinformatics

Computational Mathematics

Develop and implement mathematical models and numerical methods for complex problems.

  • Numerical Analysis
  • Differential Equations
  • Optimization Methods
  • Scientific Computing

Computational Chemistry

Study chemical systems and reactions through computational modeling and simulation.

  • Molecular Modeling
  • Reaction Kinetics
  • Drug Design
  • Quantum Chemistry

All participants will receive comprehensive training in all four computational science tracks, providing a strong foundation in scientific computing across multiple disciplines.
